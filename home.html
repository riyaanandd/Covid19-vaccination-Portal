Welcome to NMIMS, Shirpur Covid-19 Portal

Our Vision towards this Portal

We at NMIMS, Shirpur are very concerned about the health of our students and our staff,thus we have created this portal so that everyone can use it and take necessary precautions to keep themself and as well as other co-workers safe.

Here on this portal you will be able to find the data of everyone's Body Temprature and Blood Oxygen Levels whoever enters the college premises.

The data can be finded out in the TODAY'S DATA section, where it will be well organised in different google sheets for different year students and different for staff as well.

Must Follow Guidlines after Campus Reopening

  • Wearing Masks and maintaing Social Distancing is mandatory for all.
  • Students must follow all the instructions stated by the college and authorities.
  • Students must only come college when they have their classes scheduled, and for every other work they must take permission from higher authorities.
